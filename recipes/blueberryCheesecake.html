Blueberry Cheesecake

Recipe Source (Recipe Keeper App), Credit unknown

Ingredients List

  • 1 1/4 cupsgrham cracker crumbs
  • 1/3 cup butter (melted)
  • 3/4 cup caster sugar
  • 20 oz / 600 grams cream cheese
  • 2 tbsp. all purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 yolk
  • small pot of soured cream
  • vanilla extract
  • 1 cup fresh/frozen blueberries
  • icing sugar

    • Steps

    Step 1

    Melt the butter and pour onto the cracker crumbs and mix well. press into the bottom of a 9-inch spring form pan. Bake at 325 °F until the the crust is set, about 10-12 minutes. Allow to cool.

    Step 2

    in a large bowl beat the cream cheese with the flour, caster sugar, eggs, soured cream, and vanilla extract with an electric mixer until light and fluffy.

    Step 3

    pour the mixture into the pan and bake for 35-49 minutes until set. remove from the oven and leave to cool.

    Step 4

    heat half the blueberries in a pan with 2 tablespoons of icing sugar and stir gently until juicy. Squash the blueberries with a fork then continue to cook for a few minutes. Add the remaining blueberries, remove from the heat and allow to cool.

    Step 5

    Pour the blueberries over the cheesecake just before serving.
